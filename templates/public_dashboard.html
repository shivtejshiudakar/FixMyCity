About FixMyCity

FixMyCity is a digital civic engagement platform that allows citizens to report infrastructure issues directly to municipal authorities. Users can submit complaints related to roads, drainage systems, garbage collection, streetlights, and public utilities.

Each complaint follows a transparent workflow: Reported → In Progress → Resolved This ensures accountability and improves service efficiency.

Key Features
  • Easy online issue reporting
  • Real-time complaint status tracking
  • Admin dashboard for monitoring issues
  • City analytics and statistics
  • Improved citizen-government communication
